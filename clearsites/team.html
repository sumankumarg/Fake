Sanjay Suri

CEO

Sanjay is a startup veteran who started his career at Oracle Corporation in Redwood Shores working in the Server Technologies group. He worked on extensions to Oracle 8i transaction layer and also architected the integration of OLAP option to Oracle 10g. Later at Amazon.com he led product development for the Risk and Fraud sub-system of Amazon’s Payment Webservice – Flexible Payment Service.

Sanjay has been associated with multiple startups. Right after working at Oracle, he co-founded Trivium Systems in India and led product development for the flagship CRM product – SimpleRM that was sold to Infosys. In 2007, Sanjay started an online video portal - videocrux.com - and sold it after a launching private beta.

Sanjay has an MS(CS) University of California, Santa Barbara; MS (CS) from University of Poona and BA (Math) from St. Stephen's college, Delhi University.
